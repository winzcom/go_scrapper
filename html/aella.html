Aella App: The Perfect Life Partner

As a young child, you looked forward to adulting. It always looked fun to you. You couldn’t wait to become independent, receive instructions from no one and take ‘charge’ of your life. Some things you need to know about Aella.

What is Aella App?

Aella App makes it easy for people to get loans, get insurance, make payments and make investments. Aella opens up financial access for all in emerging markets – whether you are a business or an individual, we put control back in your hands with simple, straightforward products.

In which countries does Aella App operate?

Aella App currently operates out of Africa.

Can I call an Aella App representative or visit the office?

At the moment, we do not provide customer support by phone or in person. Please send us a message via the app at any time, and we will help you with a resolution. You can also send an email to support@aellacredit.com with your name, email address and details of the issue you want resolved.

How long does it take to process an Aella Credit loan?

The whole process can be completed in 5 minutes. Once your application has been completed and you accept the loan offer provided, your information will be reviewed and the decision on the loan will typically be made within minutes. If approved, funds will be received in 5 minutes of the process being completed.
